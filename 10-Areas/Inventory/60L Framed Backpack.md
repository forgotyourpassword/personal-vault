# Field & Stream Backpack (Work From the Woods Bag)

Tags: #inventory #backpack #camping #workfromthewoods

## Overview
Large capacity framed backpack — the primary "work from the woods" bag for remote work and outdoor day sessions. ~28 inches tall.

## Specs
- **Brand:** Field & Stream (est. 1871)
- **Height:** ~27-28 inches
- **Frame:** Internal frame
- **Materials:** Ripstop nylon (lid/bottom), smooth synthetic main body, tan/olive colorway
- **Hip Belt Pockets:** Yes (zippered)
- **Side Pockets:** Yes (water bottle pockets)
- **Top Lid:** Zippered brain pocket
- **Front Pocket:** Large shove-it / open-top panel pocket
- **Attachment Points:** Trekking pole loops, D-rings, daisy chains, haul loop
- **Condition:** Good, used

## Current Pack Weight
- **Base (packed, no water/food):** ~26 lbs (as of April 2026)

## Notes
- This is the "work from the woods bag" — all trail/remote work gear lives in or is associated with this pack
- Recommended for carrying full remote work kit (power station + Starlink + solar panel = ~20+ lbs of tech)
- More comfortable hip transfer for heavy loads vs. the [[Osprey Manta 34]]
