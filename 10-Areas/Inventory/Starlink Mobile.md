# Starlink Mobile

Tags: #inventory #electronics #connectivity #camping

## Overview
Portable satellite internet dish and router for use anywhere with clear sky view.

## Specs
- **Type:** Starlink Mobile (portable, not fixed address)
- **Power Draw:** 50-75W (dish + router combined)
- **Latency:** 20-60ms typical
- **Dish Size:** ~12 inches across (Generation 3 flat rectangular)
- **Weight:** ~9.5 lbs total (dish + router + cable)

## Setup Notes
- Requires Starlink app (iOS/Android) for initial setup and obstruction check
- Dish auto-orients itself — just set flat or on kickstand, plug in, wait 5-20 min
- Needs ~100 degree clear view of northern sky
- First boot: downloads firmware, scans for satellites (can take up to 20 min)
- Do initial activation at home before first field use
- Check coverage: starlink.com/map

- Any off-grid location needing broadband
