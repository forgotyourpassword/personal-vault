# EnginStar 300W Power Station

Tags: #inventory #electronics #power #camping

## Overview
Portable power station for off-grid and outdoor use.

## Specs
- **Capacity:** 296Wh / 80,000mAh
- **AC Output:** 300W continuous, pure sine wave (110V)
- **USB-C PD:** 60W
- **USB-A:** QC 3.0
- **DC Input:** 18V (solar panel compatible)
- **Weight:** ~7.5 lbs / 3.4kg
- **Brand:** EnginStar
- **Amazon ASIN:** B0FJD7LCY4

## Use Cases
- Powering MacBook Air M4 (via USB-C, ~10-15hr battery-only runtime)
- Running Starlink router + dish (~3-3.5hr without solar)
- Combined MacBook + Starlink: ~90W draw, ~3hrs battery-only

## Notes
- Pairs with [[EnginStar 60W Foldable Solar Panel]] via DC input
- In good sun: solar nearly offsets combined MacBook + Starlink draw
- Keep off damp ground (use tarp or footprint)
