# Osprey Manta 34

Tags: #inventory #backpack #hiking #camping

## Overview
Hydration daypack for trail use.

## Specs
- **Volume:** 34L
- **Type:** Ventilated back panel (trampoline mesh), hydration compatible
- **Hydration Reservoir:** Included (2.5L Hydraulics reservoir)
- **Weight:** ~3 lbs / 1.4kg
- **Hip Belt Pockets:** Yes
- **Rain Cover:** Included

## Best For
- Day hikes and trail runs
- Light to moderate loads
- Trips where back ventilation matters (hot weather)

## Not Ideal For
- Heavy tech loads (power station + Starlink = 20+ lbs, near comfort limit)
- Multi-day overnight trips

## Notes
- Use the [[60L Framed Backpack]] for heavy tech setups like the Loch Raven remote work kit
