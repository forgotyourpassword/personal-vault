# EnginStar 60W Foldable Solar Panel

Tags: #inventory #electronics #power #camping #solar

## Overview
Portable foldable solar panel for charging power stations outdoors.

## Specs
- **Output:** 60W max
- **DC Output:** 18V (for compatible power stations)
- **USB:** QC 3.0
- **Weight:** ~4 lbs
- **Brand:** EnginStar
- **Amazon ASIN:** B0B21CK3N1
- **Compatible with:** EnginStar, Jackery, Rockpals, Flashfish stations

## Use Cases
- Charging [[EnginStar 300W Power Station]] via DC input
- Direct USB charging (phone, accessories)

## Notes
- In full direct sun: 40-50W usable output
- In partial shade / forest: expect 20-35W average
- Prop at angle with bungee cord for better solar harvest
- Folds flat — strap to outside of pack or lay inside 60L bag
