**Coordinates:** 39.4225, -76.4753
**Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/SstpZ15qkQy2Bqjb8
**Plus Code:** CGFF+2V4 Perry Hall, Maryland
**Distance from home:** Walkable

**Connectivity:**
- Starlink Mobile — decent sky view available, avoid heavy tree canopy
- Cell backup — good Verizon/AT&T coverage near Perry Hall/White Marsh

**Notes:**
- Popular fly fishing stretch, flat rocky areas along the bank
- Find open clearing or elevated bank for best Starlink sky view
- Shade recommended for screen visibility
- Use headset on calls — wind near water can be noisy

## Jerusalem Mill — Gunpowder Falls State Park

**Coordinates:** 39.4631, -76.3890
**Address:** 2813 Jerusalem Rd, Kingsville, MD
**Google Maps:** https://www.google.com/maps/place/Jerusalem+Mill/
**Area:** Little Gunpowder Falls, historic Quaker village

**Connectivity:**
- Starlink Mobile — more open terrain than lower river, better sky view potential
- Cell backup — check coverage at Jerusalem Rd / Kingsville area (more rural than Perry Hall)

**Notes:**
- Historic preserved village with open meadow areas near the mill
- Good mix of open ground and tree cover — find a meadow edge for Starlink
- Parking available at the mill/park headquarters
- More remote feel than lower Gunpowder spot
- Park opens 7 AM on weekends, check weekday hours

**Spots within this area:**

Spot 1 — Jerusalem Mill Meadow (~0.1 mi from parking)
- Open ground right at the mill with mature trees at the edges
- Hang hammock at forest edge, Starlink in the meadow clearing
- Easiest setup, lowest commitment — great for first visit
- Most visited of the three spots

Spot 2 — Little Gunpowder Trail Riverbank (~0.5-1 mi)
- Trail follows river in both directions from the mill
- Find a river bend with open water view for unobstructed Starlink sky
- Big hardwoods (oak, tulip poplar) along the bank for hammock
- Adjustable distance — trail runs for miles, pick your solitude level

Spot 3 — Power Line Corridor (~0.6 mi NW of mill)
- Best Starlink option — wide cleared corridor, completely open sky
- Hang hammock at tree line on either side of the corridor
- Most solitude, best connectivity, slightly longer hike in
- Coordinates approx: 39.468, -76.397
