## What this is

This packet gives you a cleaner People-component reference for EOS work at Harbor View:
- job descriptions define the seat
- A-Player scorecards define what winning looks like in the seat

Use this when you need to think about:
- right person, right seat
- accountability chart design
- role progression

## Fast read on the seats

- **Staff Consultant:** entry-level delivery seat. Learns fast, executes accurately, builds consulting muscle.
- **Senior Consultant:** owns more day-to-day delivery, mentors juniors, starts carrying leadership weight.
- **Manager:** owns client outcomes, team leadership, and some business development. This is a real leadership seat, not just a better technician.
- **Senior Manager:** higher-stakes delivery leadership with broader portfolio ownership and stronger firm-building expectations.
- **Principal:** bridge seat between Senior Manager and Partner. Less about doing more work personally, more about building business, leaders, and leverage.
- **Partner:** practice-building, rainmaking, leadership, reputation, and firm-shaping seat.
- **Bookkeeper:** operational backbone for outsourced accounting engagements. Different track, still a real seat with clean expectations.
