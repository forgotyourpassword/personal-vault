## Quick drafting observations
- Client restriction includes prospective clients, which may be broader than ideal under more recent Maryland case-law trends.
- Employee restriction applies to any employee or contractor of the firm, not just people with whom the employee had direct working relationships.
- Confidentiality language is broad and does not appear to include modern carve-outs for protected activity, government reporting, or trade-secret whistleblower immunity.
- Useful as a prior Harbor View baseline, but not something to reuse blindly without tightening.
