## What this is
Harbor View Consulting benefits enrollment guide for the plan year 2026-02-01 through 2027-01-31.

## Key facts captured
- Effective plan year: February 1, 2026 through January 31, 2027.
- Eligibility: full-time employees working 30+ hours/week.
- Eligible dependents for medical, dental, and vision: spouses and children under 26.
- Open enrollment elections become effective 2026-02-01.
- New hires: benefits effective the first of the month following date of hire.
- Enrollment platform timing noted in the guide: new hires enroll within 30 days of hire; qualifying event changes within 30 days of the event.
- Broker / support contact: Evelyn Torbit, 443-739-9878, etorbit@wearebeneficent.com.

## Medical plans noted
Harbor View offers three open-access medical plans:
1. BlueChoice Advantage National HSA $3,400
   - Out-of-state employees only.
2. BlueChoice HMO HSA $3,400
   - MD, DC, and Northern Virginia employees.
3. BlueChoice HMO $6,900
   - MD, DC, and Northern Virginia employees.

Notes from the guide:
- No referral required between providers.
- HSA plans emphasize lower premiums, higher upfront out-of-pocket exposure, portability, and tax advantages.
- 2026 HSA contribution limits listed in the guide: $4,400 individual / $8,750 family, plus $1,000 catch-up for age 55+.

## Dental plan notes
- Preventive services covered at 100% in network.
- Deductible: $25 individual / $75 family for basic and major services.
- Basic and major surgical services covered at 80% up to the plan max.
- Major restorative services covered at 50% up to the plan max.
- Child orthodontia: 50% to $1,200 maximum.
- Annual maximum: $1,500.

## Vision plan notes
- Routine eye exam: $0 copay.
- Materials: $20 copay.
- Davis Vision collection frames: $0 copay for 200+ frames.
- Non-collection frames: plan pays up to $130 wholesale.
- Contact lenses: plan pays up to $130 unless medically necessary.
- Scratch-resistant coating included.
- Blue light coating: $15. UV coating: $12.

## Employer contribution note
- Guide states Harbor View pays 100% of the employee premium for medical, dental, and vision.
- Dependent premiums are shown in the PDF table on the "Your Cost" page.

## Network / provider lookup
- Medical network: BlueChoice or BlueChoice Advantage.
- Dental network: BlueDental Plus.
- Vision network: BlueVision Plus.
- CareFirst provider lookup referenced in the guide.
