# EOS Campaign Map

This is the more hardcore version. Instead of "read book, feel smart," this turns *Traction* into a chapter-by-chapter campaign.

## Campaign Structure
Each chapter or major section becomes a zone.

Clear a zone by doing 3 things:
1. **Lore**: summarize the chapter from memory.
2. **Combat**: answer scenario questions without peeking.
3. **Crafting**: build one real-world artifact or apply one lesson.

## Arc I: The Disorder Frontier
### Zone 1: Why Businesses Get Messy
**Theme:** growth creates complexity, noise, and drift.

**Mission Objectives**
- Explain why organizations naturally lose alignment as they grow.
- Name the 6 key components from memory.
- Write one paragraph on where complexity is currently winning in your world.

**Crafting Challenge**
- Add one note to [[00-Inbox/Traction]] called "Where complexity is taxing Harbor View."

**Reward**
- **+20 XP**
- Badge: **Fog Walker**

### Zone 2: Vision Keep
**Theme:** people cannot align around what nobody has made clear.

**Mission Objectives**
- Explain what Vision solves.
- Write a plain-English description of what a team needs to know to row in the same direction.
- Identify one place where repeated confusion is really unclear vision.

**Crafting Challenge**
- Draft a mini vision statement for a team, project, or area.

**Reward**
- **+20 XP**
- Badge: **Standard Bearer**

### Zone 3: People Gate
**Theme:** right person, right seat.

**Mission Objectives**
- Explain the difference between values fit and seat fit.
- Identify one recurring pain that smells like wrong seat or fuzzy accountability.
- Explain why titles can hide problems.

**Crafting Challenge**
- Sketch a seat-based structure in bullets.

**Reward**
- **+25 XP**
- Badge: **Seat Seer**

## Arc II: The Control Board
### Zone 4: Data Tower
**Theme:** if you cannot see it weekly, you will feel it late.

**Mission Objectives**
- Explain why scorecards matter.
- Identify 5-7 weekly numbers that would reduce surprise.
- Distinguish signal from vanity.

**Crafting Challenge**
- Draft one EOS-style scorecard.

**Reward**
- **+25 XP**
- Badge: **Signal Keeper**

### Zone 5: Issue Caverns
**Theme:** teams discuss symptoms and call it problem-solving.

**Mission Objectives**
- Define IDS from memory.
- Diagnose root issue vs symptom in 3 prompts.
- Name one issue in your world that needs cleaner framing.

**Crafting Challenge**
- Run a mini IDS on one real issue.

**Reward**
- **+30 XP**
- Badge: **Issue Hunter**

### Zone 6: Process Forge
**Theme:** consistency should live in a few core processes, not a bureaucracy swamp.

**Mission Objectives**
- Explain why EOS wants documented core processes but not endless SOP sprawl.
- Identify one repeatable process worth documenting.
- Recall the 3-7 step rule from your notes.

**Crafting Challenge**
- Draft one one-page process.

**Reward**
- **+25 XP**
- Badge: **Forgewright**

## Arc III: Traction and Command
### Zone 7: Rock Quarry
**Theme:** a few real priorities beat many fake ones.

**Mission Objectives**
- Explain Rocks in plain English.
- Convert 5 vague goals into Rocks, projects, habits, or wishes.
- Name one Rock you would set for work and one for personal systems.

**Crafting Challenge**
- Build a 90-day Rock list.

**Reward**
- **+25 XP**
- Badge: **Quartermaster**

### Zone 8: Meeting Hall
**Theme:** the weekly meeting exists to drive traction, not to host theater.

**Mission Objectives**
- Explain what belongs in a strong weekly EOS meeting.
- Diagnose why a bad meeting stays bad.
- Outline a cleaner meeting flow.

**Crafting Challenge**
- Redesign one standing meeting you hate.

**Reward**
- **+30 XP**
- Badge: **Hallbreaker**

### Zone 9: Field Command
**Theme:** use EOS in live fire, not just in notes.

**Mission Objectives**
- Diagnose a real operating issue at Harbor View.
- Choose the highest-leverage EOS tool.
- Design a 30-day experiment.

**Crafting Challenge**
- Write your answer using [[EOS Harbor View Boss Fights]].

**Reward**
- **+40 XP**
- Badge: **Field Commander**

## Final Raid: The Traction Trial
To clear the campaign:
- complete at least 6 zones
- defeat 2 standard boss fights
- defeat 1 Harbor View boss fight
- create 2 real EOS artifacts
- explain EOS simply in one page without sounding like a consultant who ate a PowerPoint

**Final Reward**
- **+75 XP**
- Title: **Traction Warden**
