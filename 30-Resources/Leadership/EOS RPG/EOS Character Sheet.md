## Current Equipment

### How equipment works
Each piece of equipment is an EOS tool or competency.
- You should eventually have **all** of these. This is not about whether EOS includes them. It does.
- The real question is whether a tool is **battle-ready** for you in real life.
- **Equipped** means you can already use this tool with some confidence in live situations.
- **Not equipped yet** means it is still a live mastery target and not battle-ready yet.
- Equipment is not just decorative. Each piece gives you a specific operating advantage in boss fights, real leadership decisions, and Harbor View operating work.
