# EOS Loot Table & Badges

Because if you're going to gamify this, you might as well do it properly.

## Badge Rules
- Badges are earned by evidence, not vibes.
- If you cannot point to a note, artifact, or session, the badge stays locked.
- A badge can unlock a perk, but not self-deception. Nice try.

## Core Badges
### Fog Walker
**Earn by:** explaining why growth creates complexity and where that is happening in real life.

**Perk:** +5 bonus XP the first time you diagnose drift correctly in a scenario.

### Standard Bearer
**Earn by:** creating a clean vision summary that a normal person could actually follow.

**Perk:** may reroll one failed explanation prompt.

### Seat Seer
**Earn by:** correctly identifying a right-seat problem and proposing a clearer ownership structure.

**Perk:** +10 XP when you create an accountability artifact.

### Signal Keeper
**Earn by:** building a scorecard with weekly leading indicators instead of lagging excuses.

**Perk:** unlocks **Scorecard Builder**.

### Issue Hunter
**Earn by:** turning 3 fuzzy complaints into real issues and running one IDS pass.

**Perk:** +5 bonus XP on boss fights involving root-cause diagnosis.

### Forgewright
**Earn by:** drafting one core process in 3-7 clear steps.

**Perk:** +5 bonus XP when you simplify a bloated workflow.

### Quartermaster
**Earn by:** converting a cluttered priority list into a few real Rocks.

**Perk:** unlocks **Rock Setter**.

### Hallbreaker
**Earn by:** redesigning a recurring meeting into something useful.

**Perk:** unlocks **Meeting Slayer**.

### Field Commander
**Earn by:** applying EOS to a real Harbor View issue with a concrete first experiment.

**Perk:** unlocks **Harbor View Mapper**.

### Traction Warden
**Earn by:** finishing the campaign and proving EOS use under live conditions.

**Perk:** you are now allowed to talk about EOS like you know what you're doing.

## Loot Drops
Use these for fun after a win.
- Clear a main quest: coffee upgrade
- Clear a boss fight: trail coffee or quiet reading block
- Reach Integrator rank: buy a notebook, pen, or workflow tool without guilt
- Reach Implementer rank: schedule a half-day strategy block
- Beat the Final Raid: pick a meaningful reward, not junk

## Optional Streak Bonuses
- 3 sessions in 7 days: **+10 XP**
- 2 weeks with 3 sessions each: **+20 XP**
- 4 weeks of steady reps: unlock **Cadence Builder**

## Rare Drops
These only count if attached to something real.
- **Anti-Consultant Aura**: explain EOS simply and usefully in conversation
- **IDS Blade**: cut to the real issue in under 2 minutes
- **Seat Map**: create a clear accountability chart draft
- **War Table**: build a real scorecard and review cadence
