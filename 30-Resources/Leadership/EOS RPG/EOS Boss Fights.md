# EOS Boss Fights

Boss fights are where recall meets judgment. Each one is meant to feel a little uncomfortable. Good.

## How to Play
For each boss fight, answer 4 things:
1. What is the real operating problem?
2. Which EOS component or tool is most relevant?
3. What would you do first?
4. What common mistake should be avoided?

Score yourself 0-5 on each answer. A clean win is 16/20 or better.

---

## Boss Fight 1: The Fog Kingdom
A leadership team says they want growth, but each person describes a different priority. Meetings feel circular. Good people are busy, but nobody can say what matters most this quarter.

**What this is testing:** Vision + Traction

**Loot if cleared:** +25 XP and unlock **Plain-English Translator**

---

## Boss Fight 2: The Nice but Wrong Seat Monster
A well-liked employee has been with the company forever. Everyone trusts them, but deadlines slip, follow-through is weak, and key responsibilities are fuzzy. Nobody wants to confront it because they are "part of the family."

**What this is testing:** People + Accountability

**Loot if cleared:** +25 XP and unlock **Pattern Hunter**

---

## Boss Fight 3: The Vanity Hydra
A team insists things are going fine because everyone is working hard. But surprises keep happening late, fire drills are constant, and nobody has a small set of weekly numbers that predict trouble.

**What this is testing:** Data

**Loot if cleared:** +25 XP and unlock **Scorecard Builder**

---

## Boss Fight 4: The Talking Swamp
Weekly meetings run 90 minutes. People give updates. Someone always brings up a real issue in minute 83. The group debates around it, runs out of time, and leaves with vague next steps.

**What this is testing:** Issues + Meeting Cadence + IDS

**Loot if cleared:** +25 XP and unlock **Meeting Slayer**

---

## Boss Fight 5: The Process Labyrinth
The business has tribal knowledge everywhere. Each employee does things a little differently. Leaders say they care about consistency, but no one has documented the handful of core processes that actually matter.

**What this is testing:** Process

**Loot if cleared:** +25 XP and unlock **Process Forger**

---

## Final Boss: The Harbor View Mirror
Pick one real operating pain from work. Do not choose a fake-clean example. Choose something messy.

Then answer:
- Which EOS component is weakest here?
- Which EOS tool would create the most leverage fastest?
- What does the first 30-day experiment look like?
- What would make this fail politically or operationally?

**Loot if cleared:** +50 XP and unlock **Harbor View Mapper**
