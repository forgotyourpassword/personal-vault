### Your Proven Process (28%)

**Key Principle:** *"Never tell someone something you can show them."*

- Use visuals instead of words whenever possible
- Capture every step on one piece of paper
- Builds customer confidence and differentiates you from competitors
- Process should be **3-7 steps**, with **3-5 bullets** under each step
- Add a **guarantee** to the service — reduces buyer hesitation, sets you apart
